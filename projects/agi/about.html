A Few Words About Us

AGI is a global information technology and educational services company that is committed to provide the market with super quality services and products.

AGI is a global information technology and educational services company that is committed to provide the market with super quality services and products.Headquartered in Egypt, AGI delivers powerful techniques and advanced technologies in IT services in many fields.

On the Educational Assessment Field, AGI has a lot of contributions such as Item-Banking Solutions, Online Testing Gate, Statistical Analyzer, Interactive Online Reporting Platform, Learning Outcomes Management System, etc. One of the major advantages of AGI systems is that they are built based on add-ons modules that are integrated in a sense to form one huge electronic platform for educational assessments. On the other hand, AGI has its contributions in other fields specially in financial and medical applications.

Moreover, AGI supports building capacity as one of the most effective strategies to maximize productivity, manage change and build self-confidence towards organizations’ success. AGI offers extensive training services for organizations and institutes. On other side, AGI offers consultation that demands skilled resources and a proven best-practice methodology to optimize costs, reduce risks, and achieve a faster return on investment.

Mission

AGI committed to provide the market with superior quality consulting, services, and products that enable momentous improvements to government’s and commercial organizations' value, efficiency and productivity, and to improve their competitiveness and increase their bottom-line

Vision

We aspire to be the most admired and valuable company in the world. Our goal is to be the company that best understands and satisfies customers’ demands

Our Company History

Advanced Group for Information Technology (AGI) was founded in 2009 as Arab Group for Assessment (AGA) to serve as Educational Assessment and Software Partner to Al-Ola Modern Systems Hardware Services.

After sometime, AGI founders decided to widen and deepen AGI’s scope of work throughout the implementation of information technology in different fields and chose Advanced Group of Information Technology (AGI) as a new brand to reflect professionalism of the teamwork and integration of IT services and technology.

Through partnerships and alliances, AGI started a new phase of business to become a leading Middle East company in the fields of software and educational services.

In a very short time, AGI succeeded in reserving respectable location in the domestic and regional market through offering a series of services and products with a long list of clients.
