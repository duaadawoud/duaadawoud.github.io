Statistical Analyzer

Statistical Analyzer is a statistical package with a user friendly interface that helps you analyze any test or survey in a few seconds. SA is advanced software developed by a team of highly qualified developers and statisticians. It helps test administrators in scoring and reporting test takers’ results with few clicks. It also produces a lot of statistical reports about the test quality; items p-value and discrimination. It assists test administrators and item writers in identifying test points of strength and weakness. It also generates frequency graphs which helps users in identifying how suitable is the test to test takers.
